About Levites Farm

Located in the serene environment of Masii, Machakos County, Kenya, Levites Farm is a thriving duck farm dedicated to sustainable, healthy, and ethical duck farming. Our goal is to provide premium quality ducks and eggs while maintaining a balance with nature.

Our Duck Breeds

At Levites Farm, we raise a variety of ducks suited for both meat and egg production. Each breed is nurtured naturally and with utmost care.

Pekin Ducks

Known for their calm nature and delicious meat.

Male: 4.5 kg | Female: 3.8 kg

Khaki Campbell

Excellent egg layers producing up to 300 eggs yearly.

Male: 2.6 kg | Female: 2.3 kg

Muscovy Ducks

Strong and healthy ducks ideal for free-range systems.

Male: 5.5 kg | Female: 3.0 kg

Farm Location

Our farm is situated in Masii, Machakos County — a peaceful environment ideal for duck farming, surrounded by nature and clean water sources.
